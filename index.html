About

ThoughtWorks Bangalore is glad to announce recruitment based Appium Workshop for QA's. If you get a chance to learn and interview at the same time wouldn't it be fun?

Agenda

  • First half of the day the sessions will be on Appium Basics including Appium Architecture, hands-on sessions on Gestures and also a glimpse on what's new in Appium
  • Second half will be the assessment based on the implementation of the concepts covered

Who can apply?

  • QA's with a minimum of 2+ years of Appium/Selenium automation experience, able to build and enhance the test automation framework.
  • Need to have a strong knowledge of any programming language with OOPs concepts, problem solving abilities and demonstrate strategic thinking.
  • Good manual testing skills

FAQ's

  • Is this a paid event? - It's a community event and it's free of cost.
  • Is my seat confirmed once I register? - The team validates the form inputs and the invitations will be sent to the selected ones. This is an invite only event.
